PERSONAL LIFE

What's the point of having good grades when you are mentally and physically drained? A school is a place where you enhance your skills and knowledge. It is also a place where you may feel comfortable, but sometimes otherwise. As you venture to Senior High School, it will be an overwhelming site to see and feel. Thus, this website will give you tips on how to survive in STEM Strand regarding your personal life.

Everyone can achieve in these fields if they have the appropriate methods and mentality. Here are some sustenance guidelines for your personal life:

  1. To always remind yourself to have a good balance between yourself, academics, relationships, and pleasure. Always put yourself as your top priority, especially your health. Then academics comes next, along with significant relationships and pleasure.

  2. Remind yourself to take a break when you feel tired and get back up stronger once fully recovered. As mentioned earlier, your health is your top priority. Thus, taking a break is an essential part of recovering and stabilizing your mental and physical health.

  3. And most importantly, to have fun. Enjoy and savor every moment of your Senior High School because this will serve as a significant memory as you grow older and experience the true meaning of life.
